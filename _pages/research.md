  <h2>Combinatorics, Graph Theory, Algorithms, and Complexity</h2>
  <p>My research is centered on applied and computational mathematics, with emphasis on combinatorics and graph theory. I work on structural graph theory, coloring problems, matchings, snarks, fullerene graphs, graph games, algorithms, and computational complexity.</p>
